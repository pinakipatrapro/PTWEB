Consulting & Advisory Services

Consulting & Advisory Services practice is primarily focused on bringing value to our customers. We have developed a set of services that are focused on improving the effectiveness and efficiency of R&D. There is one consulting service, the Strategic Training Assessment, that is normally not focused on existing customers but instead organizations who wants to transforms training and how to utilize outsourcing in their service delivery. 

Our Services

  1. Content Development Assessment  Our Content Development Assessment helps organizations improve the effectiveness and efficiency of content development capability using outsourcing as a tool. The process assesses the current state of the training content capability and process in an organization and provides roadmap to help move to the desired future state of higher effectiveness and efficiency by utilizing outsourcing.  
  2. Training Administration Assessment  If an organization has implemented a learning shared services model or is considering implementing it, then our Training Administration Assessment can help them define the strategy and road map for creating an optimized training administration function.  
  3. Training Delivery Assessment If a significant part of an organization’s training is instructor-led, training delivery costs are high, trainer staffing is not flexible enough to adjust to all demands, trainer utilization is low, trainer quality is not standard or training delivery is not the organization’s core competence, then the our Training Delivery Assessment can help organizations define their strategy and road map for creating an optimized training delivery function
  4. Third-Party Training Sourcing Assessment  Vendor-provided training is the largest unmanaged spend within a training department. Our Third-Party Training Sourcing Assessment assesses the current state of sourcing training services in organization and provides solution roadmap to move to the desired future state of better managed third-party sourcing spend by utilizing outsourcing.  
  5. Portfolio Transformation  Over time many companies have built large collections of training that rest inside curricula, catalogs, and the LMS. At any point in time, some of these programs have very targeted functional and business purposes and some are required for legal or internal policy compliance. Others, however, are out of date or have no or low utilization or are duplicative (similar content from different vendors or internally developed programs covering same topic in different parts of the organization). Still others may be libraries or collections of professional and technical skills courses with no specific business objective but available generally to support ongoing employee development. Our Learning Portfolio Transformation services helps re-design, re-configure and transform learning portfolios such that they are relevant, are linked to the business and deemed effective and efficient.  
  6. Value-Added Consulting  While our Delivery teams are focused on delivering to the contracted SLAs and ensuring service excellence, we offers a Value Added Consulting service which offers an on-demand aligned consultant for more strategic solutions to value add. Our Value-Added Consulting Services enables customers to find learning solutions, even in areas that are beyond the scope of the current contract & scope, seek strategic support to implement Managed Training Services & research and take an innovative and consultative approach to help achieve desired goals & mission 
  7. Strategic Training Assessment  The Strategic Training Assessment is a consulting service that served customers who might be interested in outsourcing. Companies have huge budget for L&D. Yet few L&D organizations know just how those dollars are being spent. Nor do they clearly understand the business value that their investments are generating. Our Strategic Training Assessment process is designed to help organization transform L&D from a cost center to an enterprise that adds value to the organization. NIIT works in close partnership with our customers to assess the current state of the training organization, the desired future state, gaps between the two and identify recommendations for helping them move to the desired future state in the organization.

SAP UI5
SAPUI5 is a framework for developing apps on the web. The engine that has been quietly powering the Fiori revolution is called the "User Interface Development Toolkit for HTML5", or UI5 for short. It's a framework for development apps on the web. Apps that are role-based, adaptive, coherent, simple and delightful. Those are the key design principles behind Fiori, and it's no surprise, as the development of UI5 itself has been in conjunction with the blossoming of Fiori as a design language and as a set of apps from SAP. Build modern, responsive web apps
World transforms service operations with SAPUI5 applications. UI5 works on ground level to design world class User interfaces, It's a set of libraries and mechanisms that allow SAP, partners and customers alike to build modern, responsive web applications. UI5 doesn't constrain us to the Fiori design language, but if we want to build according to Fiori principles including HTML and other languages, there's nothing that comes close to what UI5 has to offer.

At the forefront of the SAPUI5 revolution
SAP made SAPUI5 platform, the SAP- toolkit in February 2012. OpenUI5: A stable, capable enterprise-ready toolkit
